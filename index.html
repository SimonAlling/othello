How to play Othello/Reversi

The goal is to have the majority of the pieces on the board showing your color at the end of the game. Trap your opponent's pieces between your own pieces to turn them over.

If you don't know how to play the game, please read the Wikipedia article about Reversi.

Controls

To place a piece on the board, use your finger to drag a piece from your holder. Upon dropping the piece on a valid position, you will need to confirm your move before proceeding (unless you have changed that setting).

If there is no possible move, the turn will automatically pass to your opponent. The game ends when neither of the players can move.
